M. Evans

I swear Ed can do no wrong in my book with songwriting but even so, he manages to surprise me with each album about how much he can do. With this album, he brought in several "styles" of music from Irish to Spanish etc. and kept his twist on them at the same time. The rhythms are catchy, the harmonies are catchy, and the lyrics are you guessed it, catchy! Everything about this album has me loving it months later in my playlist. Yet another great album from Ed. Love everything about him!
