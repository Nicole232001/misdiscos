Jane E. O Connor

24 octubre 2014

This was a wonderful soundtrack with such a lively, upbeat score for a movie like "Saturday Night Fever". The 1970s/1980s were a hard time for New York City, & I think the general public looked for something that got them dancing, instead of lamenting the increasing degradation of their (& my) city. I was very interested, as well, to find out that, if you are in a position where you have to administer CPR to save someone's life, the beat of "Staying Alive" was recommended as the beat to follow in this procedure! (That makes the song title so apt! Remember this, everybody!) I confess to having happy memories come back every time I listen to this album.
